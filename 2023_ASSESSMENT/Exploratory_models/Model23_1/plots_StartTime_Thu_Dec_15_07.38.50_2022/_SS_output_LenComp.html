Length comps, whole catch, trawl (plot 1 of 2).

'N input' is the input sample size. 'N adj.' is the sample size after adjustment by the Dirichlet-Multinomial Θ parameter based on the formula N adj. = 1 / (1+Θ) + N * Θ / (1+Θ).

For this fleet, Θ = 21341.099 and the sample size multiplier is approximately Θ / (1+Θ) = 1

For more info, see

Thorson, J.T., Johnson, K.F., Methot, R.D. and Taylor, I.G. 2017. Model-based estimates of effective sample size in stock assessment models using the Dirichlet-multinomial distribution. Fisheries Research192: 84-93. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.fishres.2016.06.005
